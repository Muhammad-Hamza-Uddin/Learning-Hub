Our Story

LearnHub was founded in 2018 by a group of educators and technologists who saw a gap in accessible, high-quality online education. We started with a simple idea: what if learning could be engaging, practical, and available to anyone with an internet connection?

Today, we've grown into a global community of over 50,000 learners and 500+ expert instructors. Our platform has helped thousands of people change careers, start businesses, and achieve their dreams through education.

But we're just getting started. Every day, we're working to make learning more accessible, more engaging, and more effective for everyone.

Our Journey

From a small startup to a global learning platform

2018
LearnHub was founded with 5 courses and a vision to democratize education. Our first 100 students joined the platform.
2020
Reached 10,000 learners across 50 countries. Launched our mobile app and expanded to 200+ courses across multiple disciplines.
2023
Introduced AI-powered personalized learning paths and achieved 40,000+ active learners. Partnered with leading universities and companies.
2026
Now serving 50,000+ learners with 1,000+ courses. Launched advanced features including live mentorship, career services, and enterprise solutions.
